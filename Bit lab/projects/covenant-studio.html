Covenant Studio

Covenant Studio

challenge

Covenant Studio, an agency formed by a group of artists that, since their first years as professionals, enjoyed creating art for videogames. During that time, they acknowledged the skills in every one of them and saw the opportunity to take their abilities to branding, marketing and graphic design. Our challenge was to transmit their essence through the online portfolio, but also their different ways to communicate the ideas they worked on for different clients in order to attract potential new ones.

solution

Being our sister company, we decided to work together, joining forces and focusing on our respective strong areas. We worked on a web page adaptable to any device and easy to navigate, where the most important thing was to show and highlight the agency’s work in different areas and communicate it in an effective way through development.
