Smart Track

Smart Track

challenge

Since Smart Track directly competes with the most important geolocation companies in the US and Mexican market, we needed to develop a platform that would have whatever is necessary to position itself as one of the most relevant ones, and with this in mind, we developed a system that records the data necessary that each company needs to obtain real time, such as, the behavior of drivers, the vehicle's state, the loads temperature, etc. All this in order to provide the clients control and security on each of their units.

solution

After the great challenge that was to analyze the different needs that our customers were looking for in a platform, we were able to create a tool that could serve a large number of units in real time, as well as create reports and logs of the same information received. Through the platform, our clients are able to interact with their units by sending voice messages or, if necessary, shut down the units remotely.
