Enfoque

Enfoque

challenge

In order to keep the public constantly connected to a newsletter platform, it's important to generate empathy and trust, which can be achieved with a continuous and fast connection with the user. The objective that Enfoque Noticias proposed to us, was to offer an easy-to-use tool for a very wide range of ages of users where they could be informed instantly in the simplest and most useful way in order to keep as many users as possible and to continue capturing new ones, since the news market is very competitive in the city.

solution

Development of iOS and Android applications was made. As Focus News is a company that competes directly with the leaders in the news market, it was necessary to carry out a preliminary analysis in order to fully understand the needs of the targeted audience. According to all of this, we developed an interface where the user has a quick and easy access to the daily news of the city, as well as relevant information such as temperature and citizen reports, where they can have also a direct participation through a chat.
