iubo

challenge

Our client, an entrepreneur woman and mother of two, was looking to create a company able to offer job opportunities for other women that, like her, their lifestyle made them impossible to consider an office job. Iubo should be able to offer these women a chance to work as personal assistants from the comfort of their home or wherever they’d be located. The biggest challenge was to develop this idea and transform it into a technological tool that would facilitate the assistants activities and, at the same time, simplify the management of an assistant for the clients. We had to identify and optimize the way in which personal assistants would do the most common tasks, creating a clean and easy-to-use tool, adaptable to any kind of activity and specifically done in a way where the client would always be informed about the status of every activity to avoid that working remotely would represent an issue.

solution

Native apps for Android and iOS were developed as well as a web page that allows to search, find and hire an assistant. We worked on a tasks tracing system that allows the client to always be informed about his or her tasks status and that helps the assistant to stay organized. The graphic interface makes the interaction with the tasks very intuitive and it shouldn’t require any kind of training for any of both parties. This project was worked along our sister company Covenant Studio to not only create a great technological product but also an impeccable brand that could reflect the essence of what Iubo is.
