Dumax

Dumax

challenge

The geolocation platforms market is highly competitive. The biggest challenge was to develop a platform able to compete with the strongest companies in the market. The main objectives were to offer the possibility to monitor vehicles in real time and to attend the thousands of devices reporting every second and change. Caution was necessary with the precision and veracity of the data since any error in the information could cause issues with end users.

solution

Nowadays, Dumax is among the biggest geolocation systems of the market. We developed a platform capable of attending a great number of real time reports in an efficient and precise way. It is not only able of receiving information from the vehicles, but also of sending data so that users could control the vehicles remotely, like turn them on or off, sending voice messages to the drivers, and more.
