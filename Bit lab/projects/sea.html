SEA

SEA

challenge

SEA was faced with the need of improving the delivery time of their students, as well as improve the road quality of the surroundings of their area. The biggest challenge was to provide a tool that would connect the executives, teachers and parents at the time of the students departure, evaluating each one of the setbacks that could arise, like families with children of different grades who had different hours of departure, delivery permits to other family members, and more.

solution

After analyzing in depth of the needs of the school and the different setbacks that could arise with the students deliveries, through the app, we achieved the successful delivery of every student avoiding the excess of cars and therefore the traffic of the area. This was completed in different stages; first, making a record of each student along with their personal information, including grade, group and relationship with another student on campus. After that, the app automatically sends an alert to the parent asking them to set up the time in which they will arrive for their kids, in order to create a better organization at the exit doors of the school and a better preparation from every teacher regarding the student who is close to departure.
