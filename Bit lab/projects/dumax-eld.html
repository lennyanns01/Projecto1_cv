Dumax ELD

Dumax ELD

challenge

Since Dumax is a geolocation company that manages a large part of its clients in the USA and Mexico, it was necessary for them to adapt to the labor laws of both countries, one of those laws was to keep the information of their drivers regulated in a real time platform, especially their work hours, including breaks, loadings and hours driven per day, in order to provide greater security for the drivers.

solution

Along with Dumax, we focused into going deeper into the US "Federal Motor Carrier Safety Administration", which provided us with the necessary information to carry out the challenge of developing a mobile application that would work according to the laws. With the information gathered, we were able to create a tool that allowed each driver to keep their hours driven, rests and loadings per working day, in this way, the data could be shared with the authorities if needed.
