Chemisette

Chemisette

challenge

Chemisette was looking to renovate its web page as well as its online sales system. The main challenge was to design a web page that could work well for every past client that was used to the interaction with the previous sales system. This system should allow the visualization of the entire catalog, separate items and create users to manage and pay their orders while maintaining Chemisette’s points and awards through their multilevel model. Aiming for a highly intuitive, easy-to-use, fresh and functional approach to impress their clients.

solution

The solution was a modern design and infrastructure, so any user could familiarize with it rapidly. We worked on an accessible system that could adapt to any device, either smartphone, tablet or PC. We developed a smart search system that could help the users find any product from the extended catalog, recognizing the preferences for every user and making recommendations based on those predilections.
